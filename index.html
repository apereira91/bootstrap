About Me

My name is Alysia Pereira. I currently reside in North Carolina, but I call many places home. I was born in Mumbai, India, lived in Muscat, Oman till I was 13 years old, and then moved to North Carolina with my family.

I have a bachelors degree in Healthcare Management and a minor in Accounting from Appalachian State University. I currently work as a supervisor of Patient Communications at AdhereRX Pharmacy. I am looking to transition in a career of web development. I hope to become a Front-End developer with the Coding Bootcamp course of UNC.

In my free time, I enjoy swimming and spending time with my family and friends. Next year, will be a milestone year for me as I plan on marrying my best friend.

I am grateful to God for everything in my life, and I look forward to the future and everything that God has in store for me.
